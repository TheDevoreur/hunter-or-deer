Objective of the game:

The game takes place in a virtual world where players are divided into three camps: Deer, Bears, and Hunters.

The objective of the Deer is to survive and avoid being hunted and killed by Bears and Hunters.

Bears attempt to hunt and kill the Deer while avoiding being killed by the Hunters.

Hunters hunt the Deer and Bears while being cautious of other Hunters.

Progression in the game:

Each player starts as a level 1 Deer.

To progress, Deer must successfully avoid being hunted until reaching level 10.

Once reaching level 10 as a Bear, the player can hunt and kill Deer while avoiding being killed by Hunters.

Bears can progress to become level 10 Hunters by hunting and killing Deer and Bears.

Gameplay mechanics:

To initiate a hunt as a Bear or Hunter, go to the hunting map and identify your prey.

When you are close enough, the "Hunt complete" button will light up, allowing you to confirm your kill.

Deer receive warnings when Bears or Hunters are nearby, giving them a chance to escape or hide.

Players must cooperate and compete to survive and progress in the game.
